How can I reduce my electricity usage?
  • Instead of using the dishwasher, hand wash your dishes.
  • If you are not using electronics, don't leave them plugged in.
  • Air conditioners use a lot of electricity, so try and limit their usage whenever possible.
    • Why is it important to reduce electricity usage?
  • Wasting electricity means that power plants have to make more for the same benefit, resulting in more pollution.
  • It's often difficult to notice that electritity is being used, so being proactive goes a long way.
