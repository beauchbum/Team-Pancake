How can I reduce my water usage?
  • Instead of using the dishwasher, hand wash your dishes.
  • Try to take shorter showers.
  • When washing dishes or brushing teeth, turn off the faucet when not in use.
    • Why is it important to reduce water usage?
  • Less water usage means less ground pollution from waste water.
  • In many places, there may be a shortage of water, so using too much deprives other people of valuable water.
